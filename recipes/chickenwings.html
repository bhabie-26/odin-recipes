Chicken Wings Recipe

No grill? No problem. Baked chicken wings come together easily with just an oven and a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 10 chicken wings

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine olive oil, garlic, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a large, resealable bag; seal and shake to combine.
  3. Add chicken wings; reseal and shake to coat.
  4. Arrange chicken wings on a baking sheet.
  5. Cook chicken wings in the preheated oven until crisp and cooked through, about 1 hour.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
